Get Rid of Stucco without Any Hassle

Stucco is a plaster-like substance made from cement, lime, and water that is used to cover walls and ceilings in homes. Sometimes, you may want to replace it or remove it for renovation purposes. There are a range of ways that people can remove it from their homes ranging from power tools to just scraping off the top layer with a putty knife, which is sometimes enough. If not done properly, your stucco removal can prove to be costly and become damaged. Hence, it’s important to make sure that you hire reliable stucco removal services.

Get Rid of Stucco from Your Walls

Do you have a stucco exterior on your house and want to get rid of it but don’t know where to start? If so, here are some things you should know about stucco removal before taking the plunge.

The first step is to decide how you want the surface of your home to look after you finish the job. If you will be putting on a new coat of siding or other material, you’ll need to remove the old stucco entirely even if it means scraping off most of the existing wall. This will make it easier to keep the new siding in place and make sure that there are no seams or cracks between your old and new materials.

In addition, if you are removing stucco from a part of the exterior that gets lots of rainfall, consider installing a rubber lining on top of your existing exterior walls before applying new siding to them.
